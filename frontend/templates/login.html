Welcome to ContactX

ContactX is your intelligent assistant for managing contacts. Upload images of contact cards — the system extracts text using OCR, performs background searches on individuals and organizations, and enriches the data. All information is stored in a FAISS vector database and retrieved using a powerful LLM-based interface. Ask questions, retrieve details, or build insights — effortlessly.
